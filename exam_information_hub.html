Application Process

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Visit Official Website

Go to www.upsc.gov.in and find CDS notification

2

Register Online

Create account with valid email and mobile number

3

Fill Application Form

Complete all sections with accurate information

4

Upload Documents

Upload photograph, signature, and certificates

5

Pay Application Fee

Pay fee online using debit/credit card or net banking

6

Submit & Print

Submit application and take printout for records

Required Documents

Photograph

  • • Recent passport size color photograph
  • • Size: 3.5cm x 4.5cm
  • • Format: JPG/JPEG (10KB - 40KB)

Signature

  • • Clear signature on white paper
  • • Size: 3.5cm x 1.5cm
  • • Format: JPG/JPEG (1KB - 12KB)

Educational Certificates

  • • Graduation degree certificate
  • • Mark sheets of all semesters
  • • Format: PDF (100KB - 300KB)

Application Fee

• General/OBC: ₹200

• SC/ST/Women: No Fee

• Payment: Online (Card/Net Banking)
